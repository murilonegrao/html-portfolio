The Best Books According to Murilo

My top 3 books of all-time.

The Stoic Collection Box Set (Classic Wisdom Collection)

It's my favorite book. I like the pragmatic way in which the Stoics approach philosophical questions about life.

Think and Grow Rich

Teaches how to direct your thoughts toward success.

Atlas Shrugged

How to think about society and how politicians, associations, cooperatives, and other forms of power operate.
